About NyayaSeva

Welcome to Nyaya Seva, a not-for-profit organization that aims to provide legal research support and monetary support to verified NGOs operating in specific states or at the national level which are assisting lawyers / activists pursuing legal cases for dharmic causes.

Cases referred to us through various modes such as through the Google Form below or through Twitter, Facebook, email (info@nyayaseva.org) are verified by Nyaya Seva and based on merits of the case, Nyaya Seva would provide the following support based on requirements in each case

  • Arrange to provide legal research support in framing the cases
  • Reference of local lawyers in the vicinity
  • Reference of local activists for mobilisation, wherever required
  • Bear part of the legal costs towards lawyer fees, travel, drafting, sourcing of additional information to support the case, etc.



Nyaya Seva would always work through any of its verified NGO networks, and would not entertain requests for assistance directly from lawyers, activists or other general public. However, anyone interested in reporting or pursuing a case pertaining to dharmic causes can refer the same to Nyaya Seva through any of the abovementioned modes and Nyaya Seva would earnestly attempt to provide the requisite support through its verified NGO network.
